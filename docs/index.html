W I L L I A M

Warm wind breeze

I stumbled upon a lovely scene.

Lush trees swayed in the breeze,

Luminous flowers danced with ease.

I stood there in awe, completely still, lost in the beauty, lost in the thrill.

And as the sun began to set,

My heart was filled with peace, no regret.

暖风微拂，

我偶然发现了一幅美丽的景象，

郁郁葱葱的树在微风中摇曳，

明亮的花儿轻松起舞。

我站在那里，惊叹不已，完全静止，迷失在美丽之中，迷失在刺激之中。

随着太阳开始落山，

我的心充满了平静，没有遗憾。
